Spell Rush

Software Engineering project — Group 12 — Summer 2019

Overview

Spell Rush is an action game with a focus on an innovative battle system that uses gesture recognition.



Intended Users

Our intended audience is casusal android users anywhere from 7 - 90 years of age with as little as 5 minutes to spare to play a fast-paced, challenging game

Functionality

Spell rush is a game where you draw differant shapes on the screen to create different "spells". Your goal is to get your attack to the opposite end of the screen and damage your opponent.

These shapes can be viewed from the games tutorial menu on the main menu screen.



There are 3 spells:


Fire beats Ground and Loses to Water


Water beats Fire and Loses to Ground


Ground beats Water and Loses to Fire



Other Major functionality


  • 3 Distinct levels with increasinly difficult opponents
  • A leaderboard that stores your highest scores
